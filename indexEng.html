BACKGROUND

We people rarely do more than we have to. When we buy a bottle or can at the store, we often don't care about returning the bottle for recycling. Most shops have deposit machines, but availabily and convenience makes the easiest solution to just chuck it straight in the trash or in the environment.

150-200 million kroner is thrown in the trash every year. This is a huge amount of money that society is missing out on, and the sum equals the amount that is raised in the annual TV charity event, TV-aksjonen. Additionally, this waste is a strain on the environment. Both from littering and recycling wrong. Depositing bottles and cans also reduces pollution indirectly. Depositing just one bottle can save enough energy to recharge a cell phone 70 times!

Om oss

Easy Pant’s purpose is to introduce a new system of depositing cans and bottles, and will co-exist with todays solutions.

A new kind of depositing machine will be placed in the most suitable locations. These may include places where many people are in transit or where people spend time, such as parks and at the exit of movie theatres. Typical places where people bring their drink containers. .

The Easy Pant machines have a QR-reader which scans your own QR-code from your cell phone or a printed card for those who don’t want or can’t use their cell phone. All bottles deposited after the scan counts towards your deposit credit. .

The amount saved can be administrated through an mobile app or from the Easy Pant web site. Users can choose between transferring the saved amount to their bank account, manually or automatically (for example once every month), or they can spend it on the existing Pantelotteriet.

The container for the deposited bottles or cans is divided into two sections, one for plastic and one for metal. On the way down there is a grinder which compacts the deposited item and takes it one step closer towards finishing the recycling process.
